Planning a trip to Australia from Seattle can be an exciting experience. With the right amount of preparation, you can find flights that will fit your budget and schedule. Here is a closer look at how much flights from Seattle to Australia cost, how long they take, the cheapest day to fly, and how far the flights are.

How Much Are Flights From Seattle To Australia?

The cost of flights from Seattle to Australia will vary depending on the airline, type of ticket, and time of year. Generally, round-trip tickets will cost between $1,000 and $2,000 USD. This can increase during peak holiday and travel seasons.

How Long Are Flights From Seattle To Australia?

Flights from Seattle to Australia typically take around 16 hours and 16 minutes. This can vary slightly depending on the airline and route taken.

What'S The Cheapest Day For Flights From Seattle To Australia?

Tuesdays are usually the cheapest days to fly from Seattle to Australia. It is recommended to book tickets in advance to get the best prices.

How Far Are The Flights From Seattle To Australia?

The distance from Seattle to Australia is 8,332.41 miles. This is a long flight, so it is important to plan accordingly and make sure you are comfortable during the journey.
