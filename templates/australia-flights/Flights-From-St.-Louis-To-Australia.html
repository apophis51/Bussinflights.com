Planning a trip from St. Louis to Australia can be an exciting adventure. With the right preparation, it can be a stress free experience. This article will help you prepare for your flight by exploring the costs, duration, best days to fly, and the distance of the flight.

How Much Are Flights From St. Louis To Australia?

The cost of a flight will depend on the time of year, the airline, and the class of travel. You can expect to pay anywhere from $1,500 to $2,500 for a round-trip ticket from St. Louis to Australia.

How Long Are Flights From St. Louis To Australia?

The flight duration from St. Louis to Australia is 18h58min. This includes time for layovers and any delays that may occur.

What'S The Cheapest Day For Flights From St. Louis To Australia?

Tuesday is typically the cheapest day to book a flight from St. Louis to Australia. You can save even more money by booking your flight in advance and looking for special deals or promotions.

How Far Are The Flights From St. Louis To Australia?

The total distance of the flight is 9,756.61 miles. This is a long distance and you may want to consider breaking up the flight with a layover or two.
