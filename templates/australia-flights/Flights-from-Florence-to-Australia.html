How long are flights from Florence to Australia?

Flights from Florence, Italy to Australia typically involve at least one stopover, often in a major European hub such as London, Frankfurt or Paris, and then in a Asian hub such as Singapore or Hong Kong. The duration of the flight will depend on the specific route taken and the number of stopovers. On average, a direct flight from Florence to Sydney, Australia can take around 20-22 hours, while flights to other major cities like Melbourne and Brisbane can take around 22-24 hours.
