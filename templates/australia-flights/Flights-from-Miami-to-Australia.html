Flights from Miami, Florida to Australia typically involve at least one stopover, often in a North American city such as Los Angeles or Dallas, or in a hub city such as Sydney or Hong Kong. The duration of the flight will depend on the specific route taken and the number of stopovers. On average, a direct flight from Miami to Sydney, Australia can take around 17 hours, while flights to other major cities like Melbourne and Brisbane can take around 19 hours.

Airlines that operate flights from Miami to Australia include American Airlines, Qantas, and United Airlines. The cost of a round-trip economy class ticket can vary depending on the time of booking and travel, but generally ranges from $1000 - $2000 or more.

Flights to Australia from Miami when to buy?

The best time to buy a flight from Miami to Australia can vary depending on several factors, including the specific destination, the airline, and the time of travel. However, in general, the best time to buy a flight is usually when prices are at their lowest.

Here are a few tips that may help you find the best deal on a flight from Miami to Australia:

  1. Book in advance: Try to book your flight at least 3-6 months in advance, as airlines tend to offer lower prices for early birds.
  2. Be flexible with your travel dates: Prices can fluctuate depending on the day of the week and the time of year, so try to be flexible with your travel dates if possible.
  3. Compare prices: Use online travel agencies or fare comparison websites to compare prices across different airlines and travel dates.
  4. Sign up for price alerts: Many travel websites offer price alerts, which will notify you when the price of a flight to your desired destination drops.
  5. Be aware of peak travel seasons: Prices tend to be higher during peak travel seasons, such as summer and holidays, so try to avoid traveling during these times if possible.

Keep in mind that prices may be affected by the pandemic, so it's recommended to check with the airlines for the most up-to-date information and booking options.
