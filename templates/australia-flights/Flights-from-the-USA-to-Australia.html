How much are the flights from the USA to Australia?

The cost of flights from the US to Australia varies depending on several factors such as time of booking, time of travel, departure city, airline, class of travel, and so on. On average, a one-way ticket can cost anywhere from $600 to $1,500 or more. Round-trip tickets can range from $1,200 to $3,000 or more.

What direction do commercial flights go flying to Australia from the USA?

Commercial flights from the USA to Australia typically fly in a westward direction, across the Pacific Ocean. This is because the two countries are located on opposite sides of the globe, with the USA on the west coast of North America and Australia on the east coast of Oceania.

The flight path would generally take the plane over the Pacific Ocean, passing close to the equator, and then over northern Australia before descending to the final destination.
