Which airlines provide the cheapest flights from London to Cali? 

LATAM Airlines is often the cheapest option for flights from London to Cali. However, it's always best to compare prices among multiple airlines to make sure you're getting the best deal.

What’s the cheapest day of the week to fly from London to Cali? 

Generally speaking, the cheapest days to fly from London to Cali are Tuesdays and Wednesdays. Tuesday flights tend to be the cheapest overall, but be sure to check prices for both days to make sure you're getting the best deal.

How much are roundtrip flights from London to Cali? 

Roundtrip flights from London to Cali can vary in price depending on the airline and time of year, but you can usually find a good deal for around £400-£500. Be sure to compare prices and book your flight in advance to get the best deal.
