How long is a direct flight from LAX to Colombia?

A direct flight from LAX to Colombia typically takes around 7 hours and 5 minutes. This is the total flight time, including take-off and landing. Depending on the airline and the route taken, this time can vary, so be sure to check your flight details carefully.
