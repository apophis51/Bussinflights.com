Colombia is a beautiful country filled with diverse landscapes, vibrant culture, and friendly people. It is a popular destination for tourists and business travelers alike. Domestic flights within Colombia are an excellent way to explore the country, as they are generally quite affordable and offer a convenient way to travel between cities. In this blog, we will discuss how long domestic flights take in Colombia, which is the best airline to fly with, whether you need a passport to fly domestically, and what you need to fly domestically in Colombia.

How long does a domestic flight in Colombia take?

 Domestic flights within Colombia typically take between 1-3 hours, depending on the distance between the two cities. For example, a flight from Bogota to Medellin typically takes about 1.5 hours, while a flight from Bogota to Cartagena takes about 2.5 hours. Since Colombia is a relatively small country, most domestic flights are quite short.

Which is the best airline to fly within Colombia? 

The two main airlines that operate domestic flights in Colombia are Avianca and LATAM. Both offer excellent service and competitive prices, so it is hard to choose one as the best. Ultimately, it comes down to personal preference and which airline has the most convenient flight times for your needs.

Can you fly domestically in Colombia without a passport? 

Yes, you can fly domestically in Colombia without a passport. However, you will need to present a valid form of identification such as a driver's license or national ID card. It is important to note that if you plan to fly internationally, you will need to present a valid passport.

What do you need to fly domestically in Colombia? 

In order to fly domestically in Colombia, you will need to present a valid form of identification such as a driver's license or national ID card. You will also need to purchase a ticket from one of the major airlines such as Avianca or LATAM. Additionally, you may need to show proof of onward travel if you are flying to a different country after your domestic flight.

 

In conclusion, domestic flights in Colombia are a great way to explore the country. They are generally quite affordable and take between 1-3 hours, depending on the distance between cities. The two main airlines that operate domestic flights in Colombia are Avianca and LATAM. You can fly domestically in Colombia without a passport, but you will need to present a valid form of identification such as a driver's license or national ID card. Finally, you will need to purchase a ticket from one of the major airlines and may be required to show proof of onward travel if you are flying to a different country.
