If you are looking to purchase a flight from Miami to Cali in Colombia, you have come to the right place. There are many airlines that offer direct and indirect flights from Miami to Cali, and you can find a range of prices depending on the airline you choose. In this blog, we will discuss the cheapest airlines to fly one way from Miami to Cali, the airports you can use to fly from Miami to Cali, and which days you can fly direct from Miami to Cali.

What are the cheapest airlines to fly from Miami to Cali one-way?

The cheapest airlines to fly from Miami to Cali one-way are Copa Airlines and Avianca. Copa Airlines offers flights from Miami International Airport (MIA) to Alfonso Bonilla Aragon International Airport (CLO) at a starting price of $186 USD. Avianca offers flights from Miami International Airport (MIA) to Alfonso Bonilla Aragon International Airport (CLO) at a starting price of $205 USD. Both airlines offer direct flights and they are the cheapest options available.

What airports do I use to fly from Miami to Cali?

When flying from Miami to Cali, you will be using Miami International Airport (MIA) as your departure airport and Alfonso Bonilla Aragon International Airport (CLO) as your arrival airport. Both airports have a variety of amenities and services to ensure your flight is comfortable and stress-free.

On which days can I fly direct from Miami to Cali?

You can fly direct from Miami to Cali on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays. All other days require you to make a stopover in another city. It is important to note that flight times and availability may vary depending on the season, so it is best to check with the airline for the most up-to-date information.
