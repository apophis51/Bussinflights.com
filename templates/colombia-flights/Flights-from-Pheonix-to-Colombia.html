Planning a trip from Phoenix to Colombia can be an exciting but overwhelming experience. With so many airlines and price points, it can be difficult to determine the best flight to book. In this blog, we’ll explore the cheapest flights from Phoenix to Colombia, how far in advance to book, and other tips to keep in mind when booking your flight. 

What are the cheapest flights from Phoenix to Colombia? 

The cheapest flights from Phoenix to Colombia vary depending on the time of year and day of the week. Generally, flights on Tuesdays and Wednesdays are the most cost-effective, with the cheapest fares typically being offered in the early morning hours. Airlines such as Avianca, Copa Airlines, and JetBlue offer the most competitive prices. Additionally, flying into smaller airports such as Medellin or Cartagena can save you money, as opposed to flying into larger airports such as Bogota

How far in advance should I book a flight from Phoenix to Colombia? 

The best time to book your flight from Phoenix to Colombia is about six weeks before your desired travel date. This is when airlines typically offer the most competitive fares, so booking as early as possible can help you save money. If you’re flexible with your travel dates, you can also take advantage of last-minute deals, which are often offered a few days before the departure date. 

Tips for booking a flight from Phoenix to Colombia

When booking a flight from Phoenix to Colombia, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, check multiple airlines to compare prices and find the best deal. Additionally, consider using a travel rewards credit card to earn points and discounts. Finally, make sure to double-check the baggage allowance and fees for each airline before booking, as these can vary significantly. By following these tips, you’ll be able to find the best flight from Phoenix to Colombia for your budget and travel needs. With a little bit of research and planning, you’ll be on your way to a great vacation in Colombia.
