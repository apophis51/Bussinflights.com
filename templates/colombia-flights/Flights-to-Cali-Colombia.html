What airline goes to Cali Colombia? 

The main airlines that fly to Cali Colombia are Avianca, LATAM, and Viva Colombia. All of these airlines offer direct flights to Cali, with Avianca offering the most frequent flights. 

Can you fly direct to Colombia? 

Yes, you can fly direct to Colombia from the US, Europe, and other countries around the world. The most popular direct flights from the US include Miami, New York, and Los Angeles.

Is Cali in Colombia worth visiting? 

Absolutely! Cali is known as the salsa capital of the world and is home to some of the best salsa clubs in the world. Aside from its nightlife, Cali is also home to stunning colonial architecture, vibrant street art, and a range of outdoor activities. 

How long should I stay in Cali Colombia? 

How long you stay in Cali depends on your interests and budget. If you’re looking for a short trip, three to four days is recommended. If you’re looking for a longer trip, five to seven days is a great amount of time to explore the city and its surroundings. Overall, booking flights to Cali Colombia is a great way to explore the culture and beauty of this vibrant country.
