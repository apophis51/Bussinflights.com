What airport do you fly into for Cartagena? 

The Rafael Núñez International Airport (CTG) is the main airport for flights to Cartagena. It is located approximately 10 km from the city center and serves as the main hub for domestic and international flights to and from Cartagena. 

Can you fly from Bogota to Cartagena? 

Yes, you can fly from Bogota to Cartagena. There are several airlines that offer direct flights from Bogota to Cartagena, including Avianca, Viva Air Colombia, and LATAM. The flight usually takes about 1.5 hours.

The safest area to stay in Cartagena is the Centro Historico, which is the old town of Cartagena. This area is well-lit and patrolled by police, and most of the city's attractions are within walking distance.
