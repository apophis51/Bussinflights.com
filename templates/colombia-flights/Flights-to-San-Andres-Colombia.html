Can you fly to San Andrés Colombia? 

The answer is a resounding yes! There are several international airports in Colombia that offer direct flights to San Andrés, including Bogotá, Medellín, and Cartagena. Does San Andrés Colombia have an airport? 

Yes, San Andrés has its own international airport, Gustavo Rojas Pinilla International Airport.
