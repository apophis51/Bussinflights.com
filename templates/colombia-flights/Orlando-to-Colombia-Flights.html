What are the Cheapest Airlines to Fly from Orlando to Colombia One-Way? 

The cheapest airline to fly from Orlando to Colombia one-way is Copa Airlines. Copa Airlines offers flights from Orlando International Airport to El Dorado International Airport in Bogota or Jose Maria Cordova International Airport in Medellin. Prices for a one-way ticket on Copa Airlines start from around $250. Other airlines offering flights from Orlando to Colombia include American Airlines and Avianca. Prices for a one-way ticket on these airlines start from around $300.
