What Airport do I Fly into for Medellín Colombia?


Medellín is served by José María Córdova International Airport (MDE), located in the Rionegro municipality, about 25 kilometers from the city center. It is Colombia’s second-largest airport and serves over 10 million passengers a year.

Which Airlines Fly to Medellín José María Córdova Intl Airport?


There are a variety of airlines that fly to and from the José María Córdova International Airport, including Avianca, Air France, KLM, Copa Airlines, Viva Air Colombia, and LATAM Airlines.

What is the Best Airline in Colombia?


Avianca is the largest and most popular airline in Colombia. It is also one of the oldest airlines in the world, having been founded in 1919. It is known for its excellent customer service, modern aircraft, and low-cost flights.
