Traveling from Chicago to Monterrey, Mexico can be an exciting and affordable adventure. Many people are surprised to find out that there are a variety of cheap flights available from the Windy City to this vibrant city in the heart of Mexico. In this blog, we will explore the cost, duration, cheapest day and distance of cheap flights from Chicago to Monterrey.

How much are cheap flights from Chicago to Monterrey Mexico?

The cost of cheap flights from Chicago to Monterrey can vary depending on the time of year and the airline you choose. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $200 to $400 for a round trip ticket. It is important to do your research and compare prices to get the best deal.

How long are cheap flights from Chicago to Monterrey Mexico?

Cheap flights from Chicago to Monterrey typically take around 2h59min to arrive. This includes the time it takes to check in, board, and wait for the plane to take off. Once the plane takes off, you can expect to be in Monterrey within a few hours.

What's the cheapest day for cheap flights from Chicago to Monterrey Mexico?

Tuesdays tend to be the cheapest day to book a flight from Chicago to Monterrey. This is because many airlines offer discounts on flights on this day. It is important to do your research and compare prices to find the best deal.

How far are the cheap flights from Chicago to Monterrey Mexico?

The distance between Chicago and Monterrey is 1,316.07 miles. This is a fairly long flight, so it is important to make sure you are comfortable and have enough to entertain you during the journey.
