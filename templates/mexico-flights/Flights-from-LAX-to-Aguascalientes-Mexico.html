Flights from LAX to Aguascalientes Mexico

Flying from Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) to Aguascalientes Mexico is a great way to experience the beauty and culture of Mexico. Aguascalientes is a city located in Central Mexico, known for its colonial architecture, vibrant nightlife, and outdoor activities. Whether you're looking for a family vacation or a weekend getaway, flights from LAX to Aguascalientes are a great option.

How much are flights from LAX to Aguascalientes Mexico?

Flights from LAX to Aguascalientes Mexico typically range from $300 to $600 USD depending on the airline, season, and time of the week. Prices can fluctuate, so it's important to shop around to get the best deal.

How long are flights from LAX to Aguascalientes Mexico?

Flights from LAX to Aguascalientes Mexico typically take 2 hours and 58 minutes. Non-stop flights are available, but most flights make at least one stop along the way.

What's the cheapest day for flights from LAX to Aguascalientes Mexico?

Tuesdays are typically the cheapest day for flights from LAX to Aguascalientes Mexico. Booking a flight on Tuesday can help you save money on your trip.

How far are the flights from LAX to Aguascalientes Mexico?

The distance from LAX to Aguascalientes Mexico is 1,307.40 miles. This is about a 2 hour and 58 minute flight.
