Puerto Vallarta, Mexico is a popular tourist destination for people from Las Vegas. Flights from Las Vegas to Puerto Vallarta are frequent and relatively inexpensive. How much are Flights from Las Vegas to Puerto Vallarta Mexico?


The cost of flights from Las Vegas to Puerto Vallarta varies depending on the time of year and the airline. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $200 to $600 round-trip. There are also several budget airlines that offer discounted fares.


How long are Flights from Las Vegas to Puerto Vallarta Mexico?


The flight from Las Vegas to Puerto Vallarta takes approximately 2 hours and 48 minutes. This includes a short layover in Mexico City.        


What's the cheapest day for Flights from Las Vegas to Puerto Vallarta Mexico?


The cheapest day to fly from Las Vegas to Puerto Vallarta is usually Tuesday. Airlines often offer discounted fares on Tuesdays, so it's worth checking for deals.


How far are the Flights from Las Vegas to Puerto Vallarta Mexico?


The distance between Las Vegas and Puerto Vallarta is 1,223.23 miles. This means that the flight takes about 4 hours and 30 minutes, depending on weather and other factors.
