Flights from Lima to Mexico City offer travelers the opportunity to see two of the most vibrant and culturally rich cities in the Americas. How much are flights from Lima to Mexico City?

The cost of flights from Lima to Mexico City varies depending on the airline, time of year, and other factors. Generally, flights start around $140 USD and can go up from there.
 

How long are flights from Lima to Mexico City?

Most flights from Lima to Mexico City take between 5 and 6 hours


What's the cheapest day for flights from Lima to Mexico City?

The cheapest day for flights from Lima to Mexico City is usually Tuesday. Airlines tend to have lower fares on Tuesdays, so if you're looking for a bargain, try to book your flight on a Tuesday.


How far are the flights from Lima to Mexico City?

The flights from Lima to Mexico City are approximately 2,635.91 miles or 4,245.10 kilometers.
