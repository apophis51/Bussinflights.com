Traveling from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico is a popular route for both business and pleasure. With a flight time of just over four hours, it's a convenient way to experience the culture and beauty of Mexico. In this blog, we'll explore the cost, length, cheapest days and distance of flights from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico. 


How much are flights from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico?


The cost of flights from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico can vary depending on the time of year and the airline. Generally speaking, however, you can expect to pay anywhere from $200 to $600 for a round trip ticket. 


How long are flights from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico?


Flights from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico typically take 4 hours and 26 minutes. This includes the time to take off, cruise in the air and land at your destination. 


What's the cheapest day for flights from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico?


Generally speaking, Tuesday is the cheapest day to book a flight from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico. This is because airlines tend to have lower fares on Tuesdays, so if you're looking to save money, try booking a flight on this day. 


How far are the flights from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico?


The total distance of flights from Ontario, California to Guadalajara, Mexico is 2,079.50 miles. This is the total distance travelled, including take off and landing.
