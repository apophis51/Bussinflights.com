Cabo San Lucas, Mexico is a popular tourist destination for those looking for a tropical escape. Located on the tip of the Baja Peninsula, Cabo San Lucas offers stunning beaches, delicious seafood, and plenty of activities for all types of travelers. For those in the United States, the most convenient way to get to Cabo San Lucas is by flying from Orlando, Florida. 

How much are Flights from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico?


The cost of flights from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas will depend on several factors, including the time of year and the airline you choose. Generally, the cost of a round-trip flight from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas can range from $450 to $800, depending on the season. 

How long are Flights from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico?


Flights from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas typically take around 3h54min. However, this can vary depending on the airline and the number of stops. Most flights from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas are direct, with no stops, but some may require one or two stops. 

What's the cheapest day for Flights from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico?


Tuesdays are generally the cheapest day to fly from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas. Many airlines offer discounted fares on Tuesdays, so if you're looking for a good deal, it's best to book your flight on a Tuesday.

How far are the Flights from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico?


The distance from Orlando to Cabo San Lucas is approximately 1,799.47 miles. This is equivalent to 2,892.91 kilometers.
