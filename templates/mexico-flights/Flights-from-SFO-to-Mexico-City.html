How much are flights from SFO to Mexico City?

Prices for flights from SFO to Mexico City vary depending on the airline and season. Generally, round-trip flights can range from $100 to $400, with nonstop flights being slightly more expensive.

How long are flights from SFO to Mexico City? 

Flights from SFO to Mexico City typically take 4h3min. Nonstop flights are the quickest option, while flights with one or more stops will take longer. Total travel time including stops is closer to 10 hours.

What's the cheapest day for flights from SFO to Mexico City?

Tuesdays are typically the cheapest day to fly from SFO to Mexico City. However, prices can fluctuate based on the airline and season. 

How far are the flights from SFO to Mexico City? 

The distance from SFO to Mexico City is 1,881.02 miles. This is a long flight, so it's best to plan accordingly and make sure to bring enough snacks and entertainment for the flight.
