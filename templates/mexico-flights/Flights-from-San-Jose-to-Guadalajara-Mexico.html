How much are flights from San Jose to Guadalajara Mexico?


The cost of flights from San Jose to Guadalajara can vary depending on the airline, time of year, and other factors. Generally, the cheapest round-trip tickets can be found for around $400 USD. However, prices can go up to over $500 USD depending on the season and the airline.  


How long are flights from San Jose to Guadalajara Mexico?


Flights from San Jose to Guadalajara typically take 1 hour and 59 minutes. This includes the time spent in the air and any layovers.      


What's the cheapest day for flights from San Jose to Guadalajara Mexico?


Tuesday is usually the cheapest day to fly from San Jose to Guadalajara. However, it is always best to compare prices from multiple days to make sure you are getting the best deal.


How far are the flights from San Jose to Guadalajara Mexico?


The total distance for flights from San Jose to Guadalajara is 785.75 miles. This is equivalent to about 1266 kilometers.
