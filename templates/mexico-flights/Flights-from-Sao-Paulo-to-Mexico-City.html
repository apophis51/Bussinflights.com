Sao Paulo and Mexico City are two of the most vibrant cities in their respective countries. Whether you're looking to explore the streets of Sao Paulo or soak in the culture of Mexico City, the two cities have a lot to offer. The question is, how do you get from one to the other? Flying is the fastest and most convenient way to travel between Sao Paulo and Mexico City, and can be surprisingly affordable. In this blog post, we'll answer the questions of how much flights cost, how long they take, the cheapest day to fly, and how far apart the cities are. 

How much are flights from Sao Paulo to Mexico City?

 Flights from Sao Paulo to Mexico City can vary in price depending on the season and day of the week, but generally you can find one-way tickets for under $300. If you book in advance, you may be able to find tickets for even cheaper.

 How long are flights from Sao Paulo to Mexico City? 

The flight from Sao Paulo to Mexico City usually takes about 9 hours and 13 minutes. This can vary slightly depending on the airline and the number of stops. With stops the flight can take up to 13 hours.

 What's the cheapest day for flights from Sao Paulo to Mexico City? 

Tuesdays are typically the cheapest day to fly from Sao Paulo to Mexico City. Travelers looking to get the best deal should consider flying on a Tuesday if possible. 

How far are the flights from Sao Paulo to Mexico City? 

The flights from Sao Paulo to Mexico City cover a distance of 4,608.39 miles. This is considered a long-haul flight, so make sure to plan accordingly.

Are You ready to Fly to Mexico?

 Overall, flying from Sao Paulo to Mexico City is a great way to explore two amazing cities. Flights are typically affordable, and the journey can be completed in under 10 hours. If you plan your trip in advance, you may be able to snag a great deal on a Tuesday flight. So if you're looking for an exciting adventure, consider flying from Sao Paulo to Mexico City!
