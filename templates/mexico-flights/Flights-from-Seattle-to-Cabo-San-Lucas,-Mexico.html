Flights from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico offer an exciting opportunity for travelers to explore the beautiful beaches, world-class golf courses, and vibrant nightlife of this Mexican vacation destination. The flight is relatively short, making it a great option for a weekend getaway or a longer vacation. In this blog, we'll explore the cost, duration, cheapest day of the week, and distance of flights from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico.

How much are Flights from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico?

The cost of flights from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico can vary depending on the airline, time of year, and other factors. Generally, prices range from $400 to $800 for a round-trip ticket. Prices can be higher during peak travel season and lower during the off-season.

How long are Flights from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico?

Flights from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico typically take 3h57min. This includes the time spent in the air, as well as the layover time at the airport.

What's the cheapest day for Flights from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico?

Tuesday is typically the cheapest day for flights from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. Prices tend to be higher on the weekends, and during peak travel season.

How far are the Flights from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico?

The flight from Seattle to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico is 1,824.61 miles. This distance is equivalent to about 2,939 kilometers.
