How much are flights from syracuse to cancun mexico?
 
Flights from Syracuse to Cancun usually cost between $400 and $600. However, prices can vary depending on the time of year, airline, and type of flight you're looking for. It's best to shop around and compare prices to get the best deal. 
 
How long are flights from syracuse to cancun mexico?
 
The flight from Syracuse to Cancun typically takes approximately 3 hours and 36 minutes. This includes a layover, so make sure to factor in some extra time for connecting flights. 
 
What's the cheapest day for flights from syracuse to cancun mexico?
 
Tuesdays are typically the cheapest days to fly from Syracuse to Cancun. It's best to book your flight a few weeks in advance to get the best prices.
