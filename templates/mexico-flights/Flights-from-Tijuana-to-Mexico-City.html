How long does a flight from Tijuana to Mexico City take? 

The flight time from Tijuana to Mexico City is approximately 3 hour and 20 minutes. This does not include the time it takes to check-in, go through security, and board the plane. 

Which airlines provide the cheapest flights from Tijuana to Mexico City?
