Traveling to Mexico City from Phoenix is an exciting experience for those looking to explore the vibrant culture and unique attractions of the city. With a variety of airline options, travelers can find flights that fit their budget and schedule. In this blog, we’ll explore the cost, duration, and cheapest days of the week to fly from Phoenix to Mexico City International Airport (MEX).

How much is a flight from PHX to Mexico City Intl. Airport (MEX)?


The cost of a flight from Phoenix to Mexico City varies depending on the airline, travel dates, and other factors. Generally, a roundtrip flight costs between $300 and $500. Prices may be higher or lower depending on the airline and when you book. Booking early or late in the day can also impact the cost of the flight.

How long does a flight from Phoenix to Mexico City take?


Most nonstop flights from Phoenix to Mexico City take around three hours. Some flights may take longer depending on the number of stops and the airline. Additionally, the duration of the flight can be affected by weather, air traffic, and other factors.

What’s the cheapest day of the week to fly from Phoenix to Mexico City?


The cheapest day of the week to fly from Phoenix to Mexico City is typically Tuesday. As flights become more popular on the weekends, prices tend to increase. Additionally, prices may be lower if you fly during off-peak times or if you book your flight in advance.
