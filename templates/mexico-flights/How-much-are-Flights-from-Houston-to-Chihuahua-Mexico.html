Traveling from Houston to Chihuahua Mexico is a great way to explore the cultural and natural beauty of Mexico. Whether you are looking for a short getaway or a longer vacation, flights between Houston and Chihuahua can be a great option. Below, we'll explore the cost, length, cheapest day, and distance of this popular flight route.

How much are flights from Houston to Chihuahua Mexico?


Flights from Houston to Chihuahua Mexico can range in price depending on the airline and time of purchase. Generally, flights range from $250 to $400, with prices varying depending on the season and availability. 

How long are flights from Houston to Chihuahua Mexico?


Flights from Houston to Chihuahua Mexico typically take 2 hours and 17 minutes. This includes the time it takes to taxi to and from the gates. 

What's the cheapest day for flights from Houston to Chihuahua Mexico?


Tuesdays are typically the cheapest day to fly from Houston to Chihuahua Mexico. Prices can vary depending on the airline, but Tuesdays are often the least expensive day to fly. 

How far are the flights from Houston to Chihuahua Mexico?


The distance of flights between Houston and Chihuahua Mexico is approximately 946.08 miles. This distance varies slightly depending on the route taken.
