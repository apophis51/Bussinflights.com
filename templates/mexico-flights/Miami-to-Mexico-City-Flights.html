Traveling from Miami to Mexico City is a popular route for those looking to explore the culture, food, and history of one of the world's largest cities. With direct flights available from Miami International Airport, the journey is easy and convenient. In this blog, we will explore the cost, duration, and distance of Miami to Mexico City flights, as well as the best day of the week to get the cheapest fare.

How Much Are Miami to Mexico City Flights?

The cost of a flight from Miami to Mexico City depends on the time of year, the airline, and the type of ticket purchased. Generally, the cost of a one-way ticket ranges from $82 to $600 USD. It is possible to find cheaper fares with budget airlines, but they may not include checked bags or other amenities.

How Long Are Miami to Mexico City Flights?

The duration of a flight from Miami to Mexico City is approximately 2 hours and 55 minutes. This includes the time spent on the ground for boarding and disembarking.  Layovers can bring the flight closer to 5 hours. 

What's the Cheapest Day for Miami to Mexico City Flights?

The cheapest day to fly from Miami to Mexico City is typically Tuesday. This is because airlines tend to offer discounted fares on this day of the week. It is important to note that fares may vary depending on the time of year and other factors.

How Far Are the Miami to Mexico City Flights?

The distance from Miami to Mexico City is approximately 1,280.89 miles. This distance is covered in the aforementioned 2 hours and 55 minutes of flight time. Layovers can bring the flight closer to 5 hours. The majority of the flight is over water, with the plane passing over the Gulf of Mexico and the Yucatan Peninsula.
