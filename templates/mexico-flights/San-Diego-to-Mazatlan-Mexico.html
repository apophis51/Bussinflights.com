Mazatlan, Mexico is a popular destination for travelers from San Diego, California. Located on the Pacific Coast of Mexico, Mazatlan is known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and delicious cuisine. Traveling between San Diego and Mazatlan is easy, with direct flights available from San Diego International Airport to General Rafael Buelna International Airport in Mazatlan. In this blog, we'll discuss the cost, duration, and distance of flights from San Diego to Mazatlan.

How much are Flights from San Diego to Mazatlan Mexico?

The cost of flights from San Diego to Mazatlan Mexico can vary depending on the airline and season. Generally, roundtrip tickets can range from around $350 to $750, with prices increasing during peak travel season. Prices may also vary depending on the number of stops, the type of plane, and any additional fees.

How long are Flights from San Diego to Mazatlan Mexico?

Flights from San Diego to Mazatlan Mexico typically take around 2 hours and 35 minutes. Nonstop flights may take a few minutes less, while flights with one or more stops may take longer.

What's the cheapest day for Flights from San Diego to Mazatlan Mexico?

Tuesdays are generally the cheapest days for flights from San Diego to Mazatlan Mexico. Prices may also be lower on Wednesdays and Thursdays. Shopping around for the best deals can help you find the lowest prices for your desired travel dates.

How far are the Flights from San Diego to Mazatlan Mexico?

The total distance of flights from San Diego to Mazatlan Mexico is 1,106.57 miles.
