How much are flights from san antonio to leon mexico?


Flights from San Antonio to Leon, Mexico can vary in price depending on the airline and the time of year. Generally, the cost of a roundtrip flight ranges from $250 to $400. Prices may be lower or higher depending on the availability of flights and the time of year.

How long are flights from san antonio to leon mexico?


The flight from San Antonio to Leon, Mexico is approximately 1 hour 47 minutes. This includes the time spent in the air and the time spent on the ground during layovers.

What's the cheapest day for flights from san antonio to leon mexico?


Tuesdays are usually the cheapest day to fly from San Antonio to Leon, Mexico. Airlines often offer discounts on Tuesdays, making it the most cost-effective day to fly. It is important to note that prices can vary depending on the time of year and availability of flights.

How far are the flights from san antonio to leon mexico?
