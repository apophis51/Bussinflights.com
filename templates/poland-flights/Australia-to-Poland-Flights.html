How Much Are Australia To Poland Flights?

The price of an Australia to Poland flight can vary depending on the airline, ticket class, and time of year. Generally, flights range from around $700 to $1,400, with prices increasing during peak seasons. Off-peak prices can be more affordable, so it's worth researching the best times to fly.


How Long Are Australia To Poland Flights?

The flight time from Australia to Poland is roughly 16 hours and 44 minutes. This is a direct flight from the major international airports in Australia and Poland, and does not include any layovers or waiting time. Depending on the airline, the duration of the flight can vary slightly.


What'S The Cheapest Day For Australia To Poland Flights?

Tuesdays tend to be the cheapest day to fly from Australia to Poland, as fewer people are travelling and airlines are more likely to offer discounted fares. It's also worth looking into different airlines, as some may offer cheaper tickets than others. Additionally, flying during the middle of the week can be more affordable than on weekends.


How Far Are The Australia To Poland Flights?

The total distance of an Australia to Poland flight is 8,580.12 miles. This is a direct flight, and the distance can vary depending on the airline and any layovers or stops that are included. For those travelling to other cities within Poland, the total distance travelled will vary depending on the destination.
