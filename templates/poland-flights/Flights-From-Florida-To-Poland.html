Travelling from Florida to Poland is a journey that many people take every year, with a variety of different airlines offering flights between the two countries. In this blog, we'll discuss the cost, duration, cheapest day and distance of flights from Florida to Poland.


How Much Are Flights From Florida To Poland?

The cost of flights from Florida to Poland can vary depending on the airline, the time of year, and the type of ticket. Generally, you can expect to pay between $400 and $800 for a round trip ticket from Florida to Poland. Prices may be higher during peak travel times, such as holidays or summer months.


How Long Are Flights From Florida To Poland?

The average flight time from Florida to Poland is 10 hours and 21 minutes. This includes a layover in a connecting city, as most flights from Florida to Poland will involve at least one stop. The total flight time can vary depending on the airline and the route taken.


What'S The Cheapest Day For Flights From Florida To Poland?

Tuesdays are typically the cheapest day to book a flight from Florida to Poland. Airlines often offer discounts on flights that depart on Tuesdays, so it is best to book your tickets on this day if you are looking to save some money. Additionally, booking more than two weeks in advance can also help you to find cheaper tickets.


How Far Are The Flights From Florida To Poland?

The total distance of a flight from Florida to Poland is 5,210.77 miles. This is equivalent to 8,382 kilometers, and is a long-haul flight that crosses multiple time zones. The exact distance may vary depending on the route taken, but the average is around 5,210.77 miles.
