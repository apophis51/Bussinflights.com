Leeds Bradford Airport is a great option for those looking to travel to Poland. The airport offers a variety of flights to the country, allowing travelers to find the best option for their budget and schedule. In this blog, we will explore how much flights to Poland from Leeds Bradford Airport cost, how long they take, the cheapest day to fly and how far the trip is.


How Much Are Flights To Poland From Leeds Bradford Airport?

The cost of flights from Leeds Bradford Airport to Poland varies depending on the airline, route and time of year. Generally, flights to Poland can be found for as little as £50 for a one-way ticket. Return tickets can cost up to £400 depending on the time of year and route.


How Long Are Flights To Poland From Leeds Bradford Airport?

Flights from Leeds Bradford Airport to Poland usually take around 2 hours and 15 minutes. It is important to note that this can vary depending on the route and airline you choose.


What'S The Cheapest Day For Flights To Poland From Leeds Bradford Airport?

Tuesday is usually the cheapest day to fly from Leeds Bradford Airport to Poland. Prices on Tuesdays can be up to 25% cheaper than other days of the week. It is important to note that prices can vary depending on the time of year, airline and route.


How Far Are The Flights To Poland From Leeds Bradford Airport?

Flights from Leeds Bradford Airport to Poland are around 924.34 miles. This is roughly equivalent to 1488 kilometers.
