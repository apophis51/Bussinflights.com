Traveling to Poland from San Francisco (SFO) is a great way to explore a new culture and experience a different part of the world. With a variety of airlines and flight times, flying to Poland from SFO can be an enjoyable and affordable experience. This article will provide an overview of the cost, flight times, and the cheapest days to fly to Poland from SFO.

How Much Are Flights To Poland From Sfo?

The cost of flights to Poland from SFO vary depending on the airline, time of year, and other factors. Generally, flights from SFO to Warsaw (the capital of Poland) range from $800 to $1,200 for economy tickets. Business class tickets can be upwards of $2,000.

How Long Are Flights To Poland From Sfo?

Flights from SFO to Poland typically take 11 hours and 32 minutes. This includes a layover, which can range from 1 to 4 hours depending on the airline and route.

What'S The Cheapest Day To Fly To Poland From Sfo?

Tuesdays tend to be the cheapest days to fly to Poland from SFO. However, prices can vary depending on the season, so it's best to check multiple airlines and dates to get the best price.

How Far Are The Flights To Poland From Sfo?

The total distance of the flight from SFO to Poland is 5,834.55 miles. This is equivalent to 9,424 kilometers.
